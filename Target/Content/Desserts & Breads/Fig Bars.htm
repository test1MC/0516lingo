Fig Bars

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2016

Filling

2 cups of fig jam (see below)

1 cup of walnuts (toasted and chopped)

1 tsp vanilla extract

 

Fig jam

2 lbs fresh figs (stemmed, chopped)

1¼ cup sugar

¼ cup lemon juice (bonus add lemon rind) ½ cup water (adjust depending on thickness)

1 hot pepper (for this recipe we added 1 chopped ghost pepper (orange)- use gloves)

 

Crust

1 ¾ old fashion oats

1 ¾ cup flour

1 ¼ cup brown sugar

2 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp baking soda

½ tsp salt

1 cup chilled butter (chop into cubes)

 

Combine all dry ingredients and mix together. Add the cubed butter and mix with fingers to clump the mix together.

 

Press half the mixture into a 13x9 casserole dish (about 3 cups) – you can spray the dish first with nonstick spray but I don’t usually.

Spread the filling evenly over the crust. Top with the rest of the mixture and tap down.

 

Bake in 350 oven for about 35-45 min, until the crust browns and firm to touch.

 

I tend to tweak it every time I make this but this the general recipe I use.
