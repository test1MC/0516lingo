Arugula & Beet Salad

This recipe was created from a taco and salad truck from a street fair in Hillcrest. It was deconstructed while we were standing with a few friends in a parking lot of a self wash car wash watching a band play. Yeah, we have a bunch of 'foodie' friends, so what...

Recipe courtesy of Frank & Michela 2011

1 cup quinoa (cook with 2 cups of water)

3 medium to large beets, cubed (red or golden)

Arugula

Feta cheese

Salt & pepper

Lemon olive oil or fresh lemon juice

 

This salad is best when the cooked ingredients are warm or even cold.

 

Cook the quinoa and let cool. Either roast, grill or pan roast the beets until soft. Allow the beets to cool.

 

Place the arugula on a plate or in a bowl, then put the quinoa on it, followed by the beets then feta cheese. Salt and pepper to taste. Drizzle with a lemon olive oil or a dash of fresh lemon juice.

 

Either place in a large bowl for group setting or make individual salads as a side.

 

Serve with Eggplant Tacos (see page 1)
