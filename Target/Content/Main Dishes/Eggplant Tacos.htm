Eggplant Tacos

This recipe was created from a taco and salad truck from a street fair in Hillcrest. It was deconstructed while we were standing with a few friends in a parking lot of a self wash car wash watching a band play. Yeah, we have a bunch of 'foodie' friends, so what...

Recipe courtesy of Frank & Michela 2011

1 largeEggplant, cubed or sliced and roasted, grilled or baked (until soft)

Corn tortillas (white or yellow)

Pesto

Feta cheese

Arugula

Hummus (regular tahini hummus)

Salt to taste

 

Prepare the eggplant using your desired method and lightly salt it.

 

Spread hummus on a tortilla and add arugula, roasted eggplant, pesto and top with feta cheese. Salt and pepper to taste.

 

This can be served with the eggplant being warn or cold.

 

Serve along with Arugula & Beet Salad (see page 1)
