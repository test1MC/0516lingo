Broccoli Soup

Recipe courtesy of allrecipes.com, 2012

1 tablespoon olive oil

1 large onion, chopped

3 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped

20 ounces broccoli crowns or 2 (10 ounce) packages chopped frozen broccoli, thawed

1 potato, peeled and chopped

4 cups non-chicken chicken broth

chili flakes to taste

salt and pepper to taste

 

Heat olive oil in a large saucepan, and saute onion and garlic until tender. Add chili flakes. Mix in broccoli, potato, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 15 minutes, until vegetables are tender.

 

With a hand mixer or in a blender, puree the mixture until smooth. Return to the saucepan, and reheat. Season with nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

 

Add thyme if desired. To add more depth, cook in some carrots and zucchini with the broccoli.
