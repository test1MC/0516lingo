Pasole

Recipe courtesy of Michela, 2014

Dried hominy

2 tbsp olive oil

1½ teaspoons cumin (optional)

2-3 dried ancho chiles

2-3 Bay leaves

6+ cups vegetarian chicken stock (better than bouillon)

Salt

Black pepper

 

Lemon or lime wedges

½ onion finely sliced

1 head of cilantro, chopped

2 cups New Mexican Red Chili

 

Sort and soak the dried hominy overnight.

 

Rinse hominy and add it, chicken stock, cumin (optional), ancho chiles,and bay leaves to a crock pot. Salt and pepper to taste. Cook on auto until the hominy is 'popped' and tender (several hours).

 

Taste for salt and adjust seasonings.

 

Serve soup with the lemon or lime, onion, cilantro and New Mexican Red Chili.

 

Shredded green cabbage, for topping (optional)
