Brussels Sprouts & Sausage Soup

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2010

1 lb brussels sprouts, cleaned and halved or quartered

1 tbsp olive oil

½ lb thinly sliced vegetarian sausage

3 medium red potatoes, peeled and cubed (peeling is optional)

½ tsp caraway seeds

1 bay leaf

4+ cups of vegetarian 'chicken' stock

Salt and pepper to taste

 

Clean the brussels sprouts by cutting off the bottom end and peeling away the outer leaves. Cut each sprout in half or a quarter based on the size of the sprouts.

 

Heat the oil in a large pot and cook the sausage until slightly browned. stir in the bay leaf and caraway seeds about one minute before the sausage is done.

 

Stir in the potatoes and then the stock and bring to a boil. After heating the soup to a boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer until the potatoes and sprouts are tender, about 10 minutes. Do not overcook!

 

Remove from heat and salt and pepper to taste.

 

Add fennel, celery seeds or anise to the soup while cooking for some variation.

Cabbage is a good addition.
