Chevre Polenta

Recipe courtesy of Hong Nguyen, 2012

250 gram of vegetable stock

250 gram of heavy cream

75 gram of polenta, dried (cornmeal)

5 oz chevre goat cheese

2 tbsp butter, unsalted

Salt and pepper, to taste

Bring the stock and cream to a boil in a non stick pot. Gradually whisk in the polenta. Reduce the heat to low and cook, stirring often, until the mixture thickens and the polenta is tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Add the cheese and then butter. Stir until the butter and cheese have melted. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Serve immediately.

 

 

 

 

 

Yields 3 to 4 servings
