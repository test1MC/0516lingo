Bread Crisps

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2012

Fresh Bread, one loaf

Butter

Herbs

 

Preheat the oven to 275.

 

Melt the butter and add the herbs, minced peppers, garlic, etc. Let set for 30 minutes.

 

Cut the bread into 1/2 to 1 inch slices. coat each side of the bread with the butter mixture.

 

Bake the bread slices on parchment paper for around one hour or until the bread is crisp completely through.

 

For the Habanero Crisps, just add a minced habanero to the butter.
