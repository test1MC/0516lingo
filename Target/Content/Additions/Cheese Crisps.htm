Cheese Crips

Recipe courtesy of Frank, 2012

1 cup of grated hard cheese

 

Preheat the oven to 350. Spread the cheese out on parchment paper in thin small 'rounds'.

Cook until melted and slightly browned around the edges. remove from the oven and drape each 'round' around a small glass or form into a cup.

 

Once cooled, in a few seconds, they are ready to add to the recipe, usually a soup.
